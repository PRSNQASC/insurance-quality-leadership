# Capability Statement – Quality & Test Leadership

Senior Quality and Test Leader with over two decades of experience
delivering and assuring change across Commercial and Lloyd’s Market
insurance organisations.

Specialist in establishing pragmatic, risk-based quality models that
support regulated delivery, complex integrations, and large-scale
transformation.

---

## Core Capabilities

- Enterprise test strategy and quality operating models
- Risk-based testing aligned to financial and regulatory exposure
- Programme and portfolio-level quality leadership
- Executive-level quality and risk reporting
- Governance, audit readiness, and regulatory assurance
- Building and leading multi-disciplinary QA teams

---

## Domain Experience

- Lloyd’s market and specialty insurance
- Policy administration systems (PAS)
- Underwriting and pricing platforms
- Data, integrations, and downstream reporting
- Large-scale transformation and platform change

---

## Operating Style

- Calm, structured, and risk-focused
- Pragmatic over theoretical
- Business-language communication
- Quality as a shared responsibility, not a function

---

## Engagement Model

Typically engaged to:
- Stabilise quality in pressured delivery environments
- Improve predictability and confidence of releases
- Align testing and assurance to business risk
- Provide senior leadership and stakeholder assurance
