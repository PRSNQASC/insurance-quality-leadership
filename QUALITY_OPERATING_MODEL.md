This document outlines my high-level approach to quality operating
models within regulated insurance environments.

It focuses on intent and structure rather than process detail.

---

## Principles

- Quality aligned to business and regulatory risk
- Early involvement over late-stage control
- Transparency over false confidence
- Proportionate governance
- Clear ownership of decisions

---

## Operating Layers

### 1. Strategy & Risk
- Clear quality strategy aligned to delivery goals
- Explicit identification of high-risk business areas
- Defined quality objectives and outcomes

### 2. Delivery Assurance
- Risk-based test design and execution
- Pragmatic quality gates
- Consistent defect management and triage

### 3. Governance & Reporting
- Business-language reporting
- Executive visibility of risk and mitigations
- Audit-ready evidence without bureaucracy

### 4. Continuous Improvement
- Trend analysis and lessons learned
- Targeted improvements rather than wholesale change
- Ongoing alignment with delivery evolution

---

## Outcome

A predictable, transparent quality model that supports delivery
speed while protecting the business.
